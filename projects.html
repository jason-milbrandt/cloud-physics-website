PROJECTS

The following is a list of current and past projects related to the Cloud Physics research program. Some are on-going while others have clearly defined time periods.

Current Projects

The following are the following currently active fixed-term or long-term projects directly related to the program that HIWR is involved in or leading. The links are to HIWR webpages providing more detail of the secion's involvement to those specific projects.

Project Name HIWR Researchers Description
EarthCARE Howard Barker (lead), Zhipeng Qu, Meriem Kacimi This is all about a big new cool satellite in space.
ECALOT Zhipeng Qu (lead), Howard Barker , Meriem Kacimi This is a cal/val mission based out of Ottawa.
P3 Microphysics Jason Milbrandt (lead), Melissa Cholette This is project on the on-going development of the P3 microphysics scheme.
PONEX Zen Mariani (lead), Alexi Korolev, Melissa Cholette, and others. This is field campaign investigating atmospheric conditions during the polar night in the Arctic.
Contrails Zane Dedekind (lead), Alexei Korolev, Jason Milbrandt This is project involves the development of a NWPl-based prediction tool (CoAT) for the avoidance of contrail-producing regions.
